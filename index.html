ABOUT THE SHOW

Winner of 7 Emmy Awards, including Outstanding Comedy Series. Jason Sudeikis is an American college football coach, who is unexpectedly recruited to manage an English Premier League team, AFC Richmond, despite having no experience coaching soccer. Lasso is hired by new owner Rebecca Welton in hope that he would be a failure so she could enact revenge on her unfaithful ex-husband who was the previous owner of the team, however Ted's charm, personality and humour begins to win round Rebecca, the team and those who had been skeptical about his appointment.

CHARACTERS

TED LASSO

Played by: Jason Sudeikis

An American college football coach who is hired to coach AFC Richmond. A folksy American, he is seen as unsophisticated but is smarter than he looks. He loves to coach and cares more about people than wins.

REBECCA WELTON

Played by: Hannah Waddingham

The new owner of AFC Richmond following her divorce settlement. She initially wants to ruin the team as it was the only thing her ex-husband loved, but slowly comes around due to Ted's coaching and personality.

ROY KENT

Played by: Brett Goldstein

A champions league winner with Chelsea FC 8 years ago, once considered as one of the league's top players but, whilst he is still a widely respected fan favourite, has seen his talents decline as he approaches the end of his career, something he is conscious of.

KEELEY JONES

Played by: Juno Temple

An ambitious model who becomes very close friends with Rebecca. She joins AFC Richmond as their manager of marketing and public relations. She is also the girlfriend of AFC Richmond's striker Jamie Tartt.

JAMIE TARTT

Played by: Phil Dunster

A young up-and-coming striker, who plays for AFC Richmond (on loan from Manchester City) and is the top scorer for the club. He thinks a lot of himself and has great influence in the locker room at Nelson Road Stadium.

COACH BEARD

Played by: Brendan Hunt

He joins Ted Lasso on his trip to the UK to be his assistant coach for AFC Richmond. He is Ted's right-hand man and available to help where he can. He read a lot of soccer novels in preparation for the role of coach and indeed has more knowledge of soccer and UK culture than Ted.

NATHAN SHELLEY

Played by: Nick Mohammed

a.k.a. Nate The Great, He works as the kit man for AFC Richmond, responsible for cleaning the football kits of the players and also grounds maintenance. However, he is passionate about football and has a lot of ideas about plays.

HIGGINS

Played by: Jeremy Swift

Full name Leslie Higgins, Higgins is the Director of Communications at AFC Richmond and is seen as Rebecca Welton's assistant, being delegated tasks, such as when he had to arrange accommodation for Ted Lasso and Coach Beard when they arrived at Nelson Road Stadium.
